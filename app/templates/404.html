Here are some examples of how to use the API:

  • Get all works: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/works
  • Get works by artist: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/works?artist=[Artist Name]
  • Get works by work type or YouTube ID: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/works?work_type=Youtube or pk of YouTube
  • Register a new user: send a POST request to http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/register with the username and password in the request body.
